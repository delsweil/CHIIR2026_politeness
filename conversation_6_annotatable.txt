What does the causing the sauce to break entail, exactly? Does allowing it to boil ruin the mixture of ingredients somehow?
[conversation_id=5c8d1bee1bb19f00019d519e] [conversation.id=-N4YVpOovp4vdSVUuFMa] [conversation.participant=user]

Thanks for your question! Whisking constantly, work the butter into the sauce, making sure to keep the sauce below the boil, lest it break. It should be silky and glossy, the butter is helping to enrich, thicken, and temper the acidic edge of the sauce even more. Sauces will usually break when there isn't enough liquid to hold the fat in suspension. Boiling removes liquid and therefore, the sauce might break.
